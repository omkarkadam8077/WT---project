What is the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal?

The NCRP is a government portal by the Ministry of Home Affairs (I4C) to allow citizens to report cyber crimes in India. This mock-site replicates the look and feel of such a portal for demo purposes.

For actual cyber crime reporting, please use the official portal: cybercrime.gov.in

Common Cyber Crimes

Financial Fraud

Scams like phishing, fake investment, and UPI/OTP fraud.

Phishing

Fake emails/websites designed to steal credentials or money.

Cyber Harassment

Harassment, bullying, impersonation or stalking online.
